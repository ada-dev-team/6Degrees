House McDonald


Situated in Paulshof, Sandton we were tasked with creating a contemporary yet complementary secondary dwelling on the 1500m2 property. The client tasked us with creating a comfortable and functional space to house her mother as well as create an entertainment area for the family to enjoy as a whole.

The outbuilding is situated in close proximity to the pool area and provides extended views from the existing house.

The main concept for the project was to create a free-flowing living space, blurring inside and outside.
