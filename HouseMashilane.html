House Mashilane


Sited in a magnificent landscape on the fringes of Bismark, Limpopo. This residence is inspired by the rugged mountain terrain and the endless views the area offers, the design leans heavily on the concept of fluidity to celebrate transitions between open and private spaces.

This alludes to a space made from and within the geological setting, and allows for an enveloping shelter which is climate and experience responsive, while also offering generous views to the surrounding landscape, with numerous porous spaces which encourage interaction.
