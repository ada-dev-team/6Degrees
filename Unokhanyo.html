Unokhanyo

Six Degree Architects assisted the artist Sonwabo Valashiya in conjunction with PLNTH in the conceptual and technical process of the design for a wall applied mural at the new Jewel City Development in Maboneng, Johannesburg.

The mural illustrates the power of women; the character harnesses the light through the oil lamp she is holding. The light is seen as the symbol of power that every African woman embodies. They bring hope and positivity in any situation. Therefore, she is seen as the Mother of Light!

The built public art project is visible on the corner of Fox and Greene Street.
