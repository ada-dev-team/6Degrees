Cookies

Cookies are small digital signature files that are stored by your web browser that allow your preferences to be recorded when visiting the website. Also they may be used to track your return visits to the website.

3rd party advertising companies may also use cookies for tracking purposes.

What is a cookie?

Cookies are small pieces of information which a website can place on your computer hard drive, smartphone or any other electronic equipment having a memory function.

Cookies contain information which a website can use to improve the communication between you and your browser. The use of cookies is standard on the internet and we also use cookies on this website.

We can only read the cookies that we have stored on your computer, we cannot receive or read cookies from other websites.

We use different types of cookies in order to provide you with better and more tailored services when you use the website. We also use cookies to obtain a basis for optimising traffic on and between websites.

This information helps us improve the website content and the complete information can be shared with business partners or third parties.

We are not alone in placing cookies on your computer when you visit our website; on our website we allow Google Analytics from Google Inc. to place cookies on your computer.

There are basically two types of cookies: "temporary cookies" and "permanent cookies". The temporary cookies are related to the current visit to the website and are deleted automatically when you close your browser. Permanent cookies will be stored on your computer. Permanent cookies self-delete after a certain period but will be renewed every time you visit the website.

Which cookies do we use and for what purpose?

Three tracking cookies from Google Analytics are used to understand how the users navigate to the website and for statistics on how the users navigate on the website etc. The information is transmitted to the server in anonymous form. The cookies are stored for thirty minutes, six months and 480 days respectively.
